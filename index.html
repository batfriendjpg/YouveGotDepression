You've Got Depression! is a point-and-click style simulation of my personal experience with mental illness, including depression, anxiety and PTSD. This webpage is meant to simulate some of the symptoms of these mental illnesses, including but not limited to, instrusive thoughts, executive dysfuntion and a sense of isolation.

In order to best accomlish this I would like to ask that you please do not use your back button. Try and continue as long as possible.
